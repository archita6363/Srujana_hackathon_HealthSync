For Our Patients

Your Personal Chronic Disease Companion

Our platform is designed to make managing your health easier, safer, and more connected. Here’s what you can do as a patient:

Track Your Health in One Place

Log your daily health metrics such as blood sugar, blood pressure, weight, and symptoms.

Get Instant AI Guidance

Our built-in AI assistant analyzes your inputs and provides helpful insights. It can tell you whether your readings look safe or if you should consult your doctor right away.

Book Consultations Easily

Schedule appointments with your doctor directly through the website — no phone calls needed.

View Your Doctor’s Recommendations

Access prescriptions, advice, and care plans shared by your doctor anytime.

Stay on Track With Reminders

Stay Connected and Informed

See your progress over time through clear charts and reports, and stay motivated knowing your doctor can see your data too.

Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We're here to help.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use state-of-the-art, end-to-end encryption and comply with HIPAA standards to ensure your personal health information is always protected and confidential.

Is it free?

HealthSync offers a robust free plan for individual patients and doctors. We also provide premium plans with advanced features for larger practices and healthcare systems. You can explore all options on our pricing page.

How do I register?

Simply click the "Get Started" button in the top-right corner. You'll be prompted to select your role (Patient or Doctor), and from there, you can sign up using your email or Google account in just a few steps.
